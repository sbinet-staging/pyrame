======================
The ki_6487 module
======================

Module for Keithley 6487 voltage source. Compatible with :doc:`the ps module <cmd_ps>`.

.. note:: The support for this device is divided in two modules. The present one manages its voltage source, while the second, :doc:`cmd_mm_ki_6487 <cmd_mm_ki_6487>`, manages its picoammeter capabilities.
