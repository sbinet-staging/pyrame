======================
The mm_ki_6487 module
======================

Module for Keithley 6487 picoammeter. Compatible with :doc:`the multimeter module <cmd_multimeter>`.

.. note:: The support for this device is divided in two modules. The present one manages its picoammeter capabilities, while the second, :doc:`cmd_ki_6487 <cmd_ki_6487>`, manages its voltage source.
